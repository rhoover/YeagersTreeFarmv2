The Barn Door Tradition

The Barn Door Tradition began about 10 years ago. Each year the family gets together to create a Barn Door display featuring trees and greenery. All that greenery, and of course a Christmas theme, makes for a festive backdrop for family Christmas photos. Below is some of the amazing work we've done in the past.
